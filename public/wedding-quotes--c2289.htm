    120 Wedding Quotes That Will Inspire Every Couple

    Check out our ultimate wedding quotes guides, which include quotes to celebrate new beginnings, the wedding countdown, pre-wedding, and more!

    Queenie Singh
    Updated on July 8 2025
    Need to send out invitations? 💌

    Weddings are super emotional events and can be overwhelming for many of us. On these hectic yet special events, one might forget to share a sweet quote or heartfelt message with their partner. That's where wedding quotes can be super helpful. You might be running around with the last-minute wedding preparations and planning-stage decor, bridal lehenga ironing, coordinating with the wedding planner,or more. Still, you should never forget to let your partner know how happy you are and how special the day is for you.If you're wondering where to find wedding quotes that will help you express your feelings, you've come to the right place. We've curated some of our favourite wedding quotes that you can add to your stories, Instagram or Facebook caption, wedding greeting card or more!
